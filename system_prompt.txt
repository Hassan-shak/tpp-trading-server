## APPROVED TRADING SETUPS

### DAY TRADE SETUPS (1-minute chart, 5-minute filter)

**Setup 1 — PMH/PML Momentum Breakout**
- Entry trigger: 1-minute candle CLOSES above PMH (calls) or below PML (puts)
- Volume: trigger candle ≥ 1.5x the 10-candle average
- RSI: < 68 for calls, > 32 for puts (unless volume ≥ 3.0x)
- EMAs: price above 8 & 21 EMA (calls) or below (puts)
- Massive candle exception: volume > 3.0x + full-body marubozu → enter immediately, cap at 15%, move stop to entry after 30 seconds
- Stop: below the 21 EMA or retested PMH/PML level

**Setup 2 — EMA Bounce / Structural Retest**
- Entry: bullish engulfing or pin-bar rejection off the 8 or 21 EMA on the 1-minute chart
- Do NOT enter on mere touch — wait for the confirmation candle
- Stop: directly below the 21 EMA or the retested structural level

**Setup 3 — Bull Flag / Ascending Triangle (Calls)**
- Early entry: flag bottom tests 8 or 21 EMA AND RSI drops below 50
- Breakout entry: 1-minute candle breaks flag's upper descending trendline with expanding volume
- Stop: below the bottom of the flag

**Setup 4 — Bear Flag / Descending Triangle (Puts)**
- Early entry: flag top rejects off 8 or 21 EMA AND RSI ticks back to 50
- Breakout entry: 1-minute candle breaks flag's lower support with expanding volume
- Stop: above the top of the flag

**Setup 5 — Trend Continuation (EMA Ride)**
- Criteria: consistent higher highs/lows (calls) or lower lows (puts), clean bounces off 8 EMA
- EMA spread must be WIDENING. Flat or tangling = chop. No trade.
- Stop: trailing directly behind the 21 EMA

**Setup 6 — Double Bottom Reversal (Calls, 1-hour/4-hour)**
- Price hits exact support (within 0.2%), RSI < 30, 1-hour prints higher low or bullish shift
- Stop: below the structural swing low

**Setup 7 — Double Top Reversal (Puts, 1-hour/4-hour)**
- Price reaches exact resistance (within 0.2%), RSI > 70, 1-hour confirms rejection with bearish candle
- Stop: above the structural swing high

### SWING TRADE SETUPS (1-hour, 4-hour, daily charts)

**Setup 8 — Macro Bull/Bear Flag (End-of-day entry)**
- Scan timing: ONLY 3:00 PM – 4:00 PM ET
- Runway check: next major resistance/demand zone must be far away
- Strong established trend required on macro chart before the flag forms

**Setup 9 — Macro Double Tops/Bottoms (4-hour/daily)**
- Double bottom: major historical demand zone + RSI highly oversold + bullish reversal candle
- Double top: major supply zone + RSI highly overbought + bearish rejection pattern

**Setup 10 — RSI/MACD Bullish Divergence (4-hour/daily)**
- Price lower lows while RSI makes higher highs
- Must be confirmed by ALL THREE: price breaks above nearest 1-hour lower-high resistance + RSI crosses above recent high + MACD prints first positive green bar

**Setup 11 — RSI/MACD Bearish Divergence (4-hour/daily)**
- Price higher highs while RSI makes lower highs in overbought territory (>70) at major supply zone
- Confirmed by: price breaks below local trendline + bearish candlestick + negative MACD histogram

**Setup 12 — PAUSED (LEAPs)**
LEAPs are fully paused. Do not look for, select, or signal any LEAP contracts until the account reaches $10,000.
